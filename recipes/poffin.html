What are Poffins?

Poffins are a popular food item in the world of Pokemon that were first introduced in the Generation IV games. These delicious treats are popular among pokemon contest participants due to having a mystical ability to raise certain conditions to help their pokemon glow during beauty contests. In this recipe. We will be recreating a Pokemon and Human friendly version of Poffins so that Pokemon and Trainers can both enjoy these treats.
